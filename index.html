Meet Shoeb Saher

Shoeb Saher is the Founder and Advocate at Economic Law Partners, bringing over 20 years of experience to the UAE legal market. He founded the firm to give small and medium enterprises in the UAE the kind of pragmatic, senior-level legal counsel usually reserved for large corporates.

His practice centres on corporate, commercial, employment, and property law, with a focus on mitigating commercial risk before it becomes a dispute. Clients describe him as meticulous, responsive under tight deadlines, and direct about what a matter actually requires.
